# Narrative

## The Harmonic Empire (or The Conductorate)

Music is sacred, regulated, and industrialized. Dissonance is a threat. Innovation is surveilled. Genres are assigned to castes. Pop is propaganda. Rebellion is jazz. Empire-approved musicians play “*Calibrated Compositions*” that pacify crowds, while underground bands perform “Unscored Frequencies” (TODO: Change name) that provoke awakening.

The empire imposes "official" musical genres. Dissidence is then expressed through forbidden genres or unallowed combinations.

## Game Mechanic Ideas

- Song Popularity affects Empire suspicion
- Enemies react differently to Funny, Raw, Touching
- Genres unlock specific card pools
- Gigs influence local mood (in Planet/Space Station/Other Celestian Bodies) and Fan acquisition
- Boss Gigs flagged as Empire-monitored zones
- Your Band is hunted when playing anti-Empire songs
